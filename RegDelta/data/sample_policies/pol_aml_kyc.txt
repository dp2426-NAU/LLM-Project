Policy: Anti-Money Laundering and Know Your Customer Procedures
Policy ID: POL-AML-001
Department: Financial Crime Compliance
Version: 5.0
Last Reviewed: 2024-01-10

1. Overview
This policy establishes the firm's procedures for detecting and preventing money laundering,
terrorist financing, and other financial crimes. It implements the requirements of the Bank
Secrecy Act (BSA) and applicable FinCEN regulations.

2. Customer Identification Program (CIP)
2.1 The firm must verify the identity of each new customer prior to account opening.
2.2 Required identifying information:
    - For individuals: legal name, date of birth, address, government ID number
    - For legal entities: legal name, address, EIN/TIN, formation documents

2.3 Beneficial Ownership
For legal entity customers, the firm must collect beneficial ownership information
for all individuals owning 25% or more of the entity, plus one control person.
Verification must be completed within 5 business days of account opening.

3. Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD)
3.1 EDD is required for:
    - Politically Exposed Persons (PEPs) and their immediate family members
    - Customers in high-risk jurisdictions
    - Cash-intensive businesses
    - Correspondent banking relationships

3.2 EDD includes: source of wealth/funds documentation, senior management approval,
    enhanced ongoing monitoring with quarterly review.

4. Transaction Monitoring
4.1 The firm employs automated transaction monitoring systems to detect suspicious activity.
4.2 Alerts must be reviewed within 5 business days of generation.
4.3 Suspicious Activity Reports (SARs) must be filed with FinCEN within 30 days
    of initial detection, or 60 days if additional investigation is required.

5. Record Retention
All CIP, EDD, and transaction monitoring records must be retained for 5 years
from the date of account closure or final transaction.

6. Training
All customer-facing employees and compliance staff must complete annual AML training.
New hires must complete training within 30 days of employment commencement.
